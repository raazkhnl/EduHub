# Next Generation Network Technologies

> **Code:** ENCTNS501 · **Credits:** 4 · **Program:** M.Sc. Network & Cybersecurity · **Year/Part:** I / I

This subject is the conceptual backbone of the MSNCS first semester. It walks
through the three big architectural shifts in networking that happened in the
last fifteen years — addressing (IPv4 → IPv6), control (hardware → software),
and access (wired → 5G / IoT) — and then turns to what is coming next: named
data, intent-based control, and quantum networking. The notes are written as
prose, not slides; expect ~50 minutes of reading per chapter.
