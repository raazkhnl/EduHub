# M.Sc. in Computer Engineering — Networks & Cybersecurity

A two-year, four-semester evening program under the [Department of Electronics
and Computer Engineering](https://doece.pcampus.edu.np/), IOE Pulchowk Campus,
affiliated with Tribhuvan University. The program runs core and elective
coursework across three semesters, with the fourth dedicated to either a
research thesis or an industry case study.

> **Note.** The taxonomy below reflects the syllabus as of our last review.
> Treat your institute's official handout as authoritative if it disagrees.
