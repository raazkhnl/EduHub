# M.Sc. in Computer Engineering — Networks & Cybersecurity

A two-year, four-semester **evening program** (4:15 PM in winter / 5:15 PM in
summer) under the [Department of Electronics and Computer Engineering](https://doece.pcampus.edu.np/),
IOE Pulchowk Campus, affiliated with Tribhuvan University. The program runs
sixteen-credit core/elective coursework across three semesters; the fourth is
either a thesis (research track) or a bundled set of capstone subjects with an
industry case study (course-based track).

> **Note.** The taxonomy below mirrors the official syllabus on the DoECE
> course-outline page. Treat your institute's hand-out as authoritative if a
> later revision disagrees.
