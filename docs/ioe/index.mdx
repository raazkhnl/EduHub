# Institute of Engineering — Tribhuvan University

IOE is Nepal's largest engineering school, with five constituent campuses
(Pulchowk, Thapathali, Western, Eastern, Purwanchal) and several affiliated
institutions. It runs Bachelor (BE), Master (MSc), and PhD programs across
civil, electrical, electronics, computer, and mechanical engineering tracks.
