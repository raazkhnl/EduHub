# Bachelor of Civil Engineering (BCE)

A four-year, eight-semester program at IOE leading to a B.E. in Civil
Engineering. Content scaffold pending — open a PR to seed the first subject.

## What this section will hold

- Year I — Engineering Mathematics, Drawing, Mechanics, Surveying I
- Year II — Strength of Materials, Fluid Mechanics, Surveying II, Construction Materials
- Year III — Structural Analysis, RCC Design, Geotechnical, Transportation
- Year IV — Prestressed Concrete, Project Management, Estimation, Major Project
